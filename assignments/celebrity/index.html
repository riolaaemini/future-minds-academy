The Weeknd (real name: Abel Makkonen Tesfaye) is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer known for his distinctive voice, genre-blurring music, and moody, atmospheric aesthetic. He gained initial recognition in 2010 through his mixtapes, which blended R&B, pop, and electronic elements, before transitioning into mainstream success.

Early life

  • Born: February 16, 1990, in Toronto, Canada.
  • He is of Ethiopian descent, raised by his mother and grandmother after his parents separated.
  • Abel dropped out of high school and began pursuing music under the alias "The Weeknd" (he dropped the "e" to avoid trademark issues with a Canadian band)

Career Highlights

1. Mixtapes and Breakthrough (2010-2013)

  • 2010: Uploaded three songs to YouTube that quickly garnered attention.
  • 2011: Released three critically acclaimed mixtapes:
    • House of Balloons
    • Thursday
    • Echoes of Silence These tapes were later compiled into a remastered collection titled Trilogy (2012).
  • His early work stood out for its dark themes, exploring topics like alienation, fame, and drug use, set to innovative production.

2. Mainstream Success (2014-2016)

  • 2014: Released the single "Earned It" for the Fifty Shades of Grey soundtrack, earning him his first Grammy nomination.
  • 2015: Released the album Beauty Behind the Madness
    • Featured hits like "Can't Feel My Face", "The Hills", and "Earned It".
    • The album marked a shift to more pop-oriented sounds and became his breakout into the global music scene.
    • Won multiple awards, including two Grammys.
  • 2016: Released Starboy:
    • The title track "Starboy" (featuring Daft Punk) was a massive commercial success.
    • The album solidified him as a global pop icon while keeping his signature dark, atmospheric style.

3. Recent Work and Global Fame (2019-Present)

  • 2019: Released After Hours, one of his most critically and commercially successful albums
    • "Blinding Lights": A chart-topping single known for its 80s-inspired production, it became one of the most successful songs of the decade.
    • The album explored heartbreak, self-reflection, and the darker sides of fame, which are recurring themes in his work.
  • 2021: Performed at the Super Bowl LV Halftime Show.
  • 2022: Released Dawn FM, which continued his exploration of retro sounds, particularly from the 80s, with a conceptual theme of purgatory and self-exploration.

Awards and Achievements

  • Grammy Awards: 4 wins and multiple nominations.
  • Billboard Music Awards: Numerous wins, including Top Artist and Top Hot 100 Artist.
  • Juno Awards: Significant recognition in his native Canada.

The Weeknd's ability to consistently evolve while maintaining a unique, identifiable style has made him one of the defining artists of his generation.
